TEACHING

I am the lab instructor and teaching assistant for GIS classes in the Department of Geography at Dartmouth College. I also develop lectures, workshops, and mapping activities for other classes, and advise students on independent research. I value teaching as a practice of connection, care, and reciprocal learning. I am passionate about de-mystifying the "black box" of GIS while centering it in the social and political systems that produced it.

Website developed with learning modules, tutorials, and other resources for students new to GIS: https://sites.dartmouth.edu/gis-geography/
