RESEARCH

I support faculty and student research at Dartmouth and assist with spatial analysis, data analysis, and geovisualization.

In my own work, I am interested in the following:

  • Creative cartographies and art as a geographic method
  • Participatory mapping, particularly as a way to process trauma and preserve memories
  • Climate disasters, disaster justice, and mutual aid
  • Integrating care and community work into GIS pedagogy
  • Children's spatial practices, politics, and cartographies
