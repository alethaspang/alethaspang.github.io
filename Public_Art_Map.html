Public Art in Pueblo, Colorado

Series of maps about the locations and energy generation of pumped storage hydropower projects in the United States. Designed for Professor Chris Sneddon for his presentation at the 2023 American Association of Geographers conference.

Process: Mapped in ArcGIS Pro and designed in Adobe Illustrator and InDesign.


Data sources: Chris Sneddon, Carlos Ohler Garcia, Natural Earth, US Census Bureau, DOI, Esri, Riverside County Mapping Portal, Washington Geospatial Open Data Portal, CDOT.
